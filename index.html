How To Play:



This game requires 2 players.

As you click "Roll Dice", the "Current" score will increase. This score is your current score for the turn. If you click "Hold", it will deposit whatever your "current" score is to your bank (The numbers under player 1 & 2). If you roll a "1" then it will RESET your current score for the round to "0". The first player to despoit 50 points into their bank wins the game!
